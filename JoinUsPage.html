Why work for SSS?

We are growing, and we believe it's thanks to our people's greatness and hard work.

Our team members have become professionals in BPO services throughout the years, from individual contributors to effective managers and leaders.


So we understand the importance of working in a place where growing and advancing is an option, and this opportunity to grow is what we offer to those who work with us.


We also understand that the future of work is remote; 2020 taught us that it is possible and even more sustainable.


In most of our positions, we'll help you start your remote work and provide you with the solutions to make your work more efficient than ever.

How do we recruit?

1.

Submitted Application Form

You can apply through the website or our hiring ads on Facebook and Instagram.

In the beginning, we only requested you provide contact information so we could create a communication channel with you.

2.

Pre-interview Form

We'll contact you to get to know you, what you are interested in and what you are looking for in the workplace.

We'll ask you to fill out a pre-interview form to formalize your request and prepare for the next step.

3.

Interview

Then we'll schedule an interview day for you to know the operation team and more about us.

4.

Client Approval

An essential step of our recruitment process is client approval.

At this point will provide the client with the information they need, and we'll vouch for you.

5.

Hired

Once we have the final approval and your tools ready, you only need to wait for our training to begin, and you will be on board.
