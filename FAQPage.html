Frequently Asked Questions

01

What is BPO?

Business Process Outsourcing (BPO) is a business practice in which business contracts with an external service provider (mostly call contact centers) to delegate business functions or tasks. For example: Customer care BPO services are about delegating your tasks of providing customer support to experts, allowing your business to focus on other key business tasks.

02

I am wary about outsourcing but want to give it a try. Why should I outsource?

We understand it can be overwhelming having to delegate and hoping to get the same success as if you were doing the tasks yourself. But growing a business is part of trusting that in your decision-making. So this is why we prefer to talk with our clients before agreeing to any contract. We explain and make sure they understand our business model, skill and competencies, pricing, and how we'll handle their customers. Once you have all the information you need, we trust you will make the best decision for your business and move forward with your goals.
